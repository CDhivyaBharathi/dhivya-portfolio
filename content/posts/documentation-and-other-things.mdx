Hello World.

I am Dhivya Bharathi, currently spending my summer at Ashoka. Spending my summer on campus without my friends has urged me to start this blog as a personal project — documenting my summer and hopefully the upcoming years (hopefully I am consistent!).

Documentation seems like a simple component, but for me personally it's my favourite part of making things. It helps me pour out my thinking from when I was building the project, like explaining it to my 6 year old self.

I picked this up by helping out my friends with computer science projects in 11th and 12th grade. I used to write long comments after every single line of Python code so that they'd understand and not mess up when the teacher questioned them about the project. (The teacher found out lol!)

Hence, this is my effort to do the same with my life and its trajectories.

Scratch Space is a word I picked up from the lab, where it is considered a space to dump stuff you want to share with others on the local network. I don't think there could be a better title than that.

See you soon.
